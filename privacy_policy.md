# FocusFlow Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 2026

FocusFlow ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the FocusFlow mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect
FocusFlow uses the following Android APIs to provide its core functionality:
- **Accessibility Services:** Used to detect when a restricted app is launched so FocusFlow can display a focus lock screen overlay.
- **Usage Access:** Used to calculate your daily screen time and track your digital habits.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
- **Local Processing Only:** All screen time data, app usage statistics, and accessibility event data are processed **strictly locally on your device**. 
- **No Cloud Transmission:** FocusFlow does **NOT** transmit, upload, or share your personal data, screen time logs, or accessibility data to any cloud server, third-party database, or external service. 

## 3. Data Storage and Security
Your data is stored securely in your device's internal local database. You have full control over your data and can clear your history or delete custom schedules directly within the app.

## 4. Third-Party Services
FocusFlow does not integrate with third-party tracking or analytics SDKs that collect personal information.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer directly.
